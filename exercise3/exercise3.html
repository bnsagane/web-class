what is a website?

I view websites as an ever changing digital space where information can be displayed in endless amounts of styles. I like how they can be simply defined as a file that lives on a server. They can always be updated and change overtime. They can also capture an era of time and freeze it if the website doesn't get updated and years pass.



my relationship with technology

My relationship with tech is always changing. I have times where I really enjoy using tech and being online. I am able to find many things online and find new interests and learn about things I don't think I would have come across learning at school. The times where I don't really enjoy technology are times where I feel like I've become overly dependent on it, but it is very challenging to escape that now because of how present it is in my everyday life.



will websites someday become obsolete?

I do not think that any time soon in the future that websites will slowly go out of use. Many are constantly updated and even those that do not, act as a digital archive. Our society now is very dependent on finding information online, which can be found on websites.
