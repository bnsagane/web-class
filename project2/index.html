What type of website is Zara?



Zara is a fashion brand that sells clothes, accessories, beauty products, and has even added a hair care product line.






Overall webiste legibility

When I first opened the website, still and moving images take up most of the browser.

Depending on the color of the image, the text within the header can become quite challenging to read. (ie. 'help,' 'login,' and 'shopping bag' buttons)








Interactivity & UX

When I click to view clothes, a large image takes up the entire display and when I continue to scroll, more large images take up the screen. Initially, I am not able to view many products at once and it takes a lot of effort to scroll through all of the clothing options when viewing them piece by piece.





Display


There is a way to change the display of products on the website in which they could become like this





Menu


When I clicked on 'woman,' the drop down menu was very legible. Since it is black text against a white background within a box, it is seperate from the images.




help section

Initially, when I clicked on the help button I was a little confused as to why I was seeing a close up of a person's face instead of an email, phone number, or chat bot
